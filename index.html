What is Laurel’s Organized Chaos?

A darkly whimsical blend of witchy aesthetic, honest storytelling, and chaotic beauty. I’ve rebuilt my life more times than I can count through motherhood, school, survival, and healing. This space is where I turn that journey into creativity, products, and real talk — for anyone who feels like their life is equal parts magic and mess.
